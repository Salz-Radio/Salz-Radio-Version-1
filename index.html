about

We are a radio station that streams out of a café in Berlin Kreuzberg. Your hosts are Dane and Philipp who are running the station out of pure passion. We invite people from the quirky business scene of Berlin to give them a voice via interviews and the music they love listening to. Our focus is mainly on the food and beverage scene but not only. We are live, from Thursdays till Saturdays from 3pm till 7pm.
